Bei Jing

Beijing is a charming city, with small alleys, old teahouses, trendy bar streets and bustling commercial districts. Infinite modern elements blend with the authentic Beijing flavor of old Beijing, thus building a profound cultural heritage and an orthodox yet elegant lifestyle in Beijing. The must-see scenic spots in Beijing are Tiananmen Square, Forbidden City and Great Wall. Besides, you can make diversified choices according to your interests and hobbies. Whether it's couples traveling or parent-child traveling, looking forward to royal monuments or fashionable neighborhoods, you can find the most suitable place in Beijing.

Shang Hai

Shanghai, known as the "magic capital", has always been the representative of the prosperous cities in China. Shanghai's convenient public transportation can ensure that tourists can easily travel to and from various scenic spots in the city. With the Bund as the center, visitors can visit Shanghai representative scenic spots such as Nanjing Road Pedestrian Street, Lujiazui, Chenghuang Temple and People's Square. Disneyland is far away from the urban area and has a large passenger flow, so it's best to spend one day alone.

Guang Zhou

Guangzhou is not only a small waist, but also a famous Cantonese food town, a shopping paradise and a place where cultures meet. Foshan, with a long culture and history, can be said to be an important "business card" of Lingnan culture. Foshan is a famous "hometown of Wushu", which has been famous in Huang Feihong and Bruce Lee, and is also an important birthplace of Southern Wushu. In addition, Foshan is regarded as the hometown of Cantonese opera, which is regarded as an important feature of Foshan culture and has a long history.

Tibet

The highest and most magnificent palace in the world is the most important symbol of Lhasa and even Tibet. "Potala" is Sanskrit and translated as "Putuo", which originally refers to the residence of Guanyin Bodhisattva, and Potala Palace is also a holy place in the hearts of all pilgrims. It is mainly composed of the Red Palace and the White House. The highest Red Palace in the middle is an important place to hold religious ceremonies, and the surrounding White House is mainly the activity place of the Dalai Lama. There are pagodas, statues, frescoes, Thangka and scriptures in the collection, among which the most striking is the eight spiritual pagodas of the Dalai Lama since the Fifth World War.

Wu Han

The Yellow Crane Tower is located on the banks of Wuchang River, close to the Yangtze River Bridge, with the reputation of "the first floor in the world" and one of the "three famous buildings in the south of the Yangtze River". Famous for the poems of "where long ago a yellow crane bore a sage to heaven and nothing is left now but the Yellow Crane Terrace", Li Bai and Mao Zedong all left poems for the Yellow Crane Tower. Built on the Snake Mountain, overlooking the three towns of Wuhan and the Yangtze River, with a wide view, it is one of the city landmarks of Wuhan. There are also a large number of stone tablets and stone carvings in the scenic area, which are the original works of celebrities of all ages and have great artistic value.

Cheng Du

It is the largest water conservancy project in the world, which is the oldest and the only one left, and is characterized by water diversion without dam. It mainly consists of three parts: fishmouth water diversion dike, Feisha weir spillway and Baokoukou water inlet, which always play the role of flood control and irrigation. There are many cultural relics near Dujiangyan, including Fulong Temple, Erwang Temple, Anlan Cable Bridge, Yuleiguan and Lidui Park.

Chong Qing

"Hongyadong" injects the ancient architectural art of Bayu Diaojiao Building into a brand-new cultural concept with innovative thinking; It is perfectly integrated with the river view and the city style, with an extraordinary drop of 75 meters high, climbing up the cliff, strewn at random, and built on the mountain. It condenses the precipitation of Chongqing's 3,000-year history, integrates Chongqing's unique Ba-Yu folk culture, the architectural culture of mountain dwellings and the dock culture, and becomes a unique and beautiful city card that attracts worldwide attention.

Dun Huang

Located at the northern foot of Mingsha Mountain, 5 km south of Dunhuang City, Gansu Province, it has been famous for its wonderful landscape of "mountain spring coexisting, sand and water coexisting" since ancient times. Spring shape resembles a crescent moon, hence the name Crescent Spring. In ancient times, it was called "Shajing", also known as "Yaoquan". The spring is surrounded by Mingsha Mountain, but it is not covered by quicksand. It is always rippling and crystal clear. It does not overflow for a long time and does not dry up after a long drought.

The Potala Palace

Potala Palace is a holy place of Tibetan Buddhism, a miniature modern history of Tibet, and an encyclopedia for a comprehensive understanding of Tibet's religion, history, politics and humanities. It is located on Mabri Mountain in Lhasa, Tibet, the highest in Shanghai in the world, and the largest and most complete ancient palace and castle complex in Tibet. Potala Palace covers a total area of 360,000 square meters, with a total building area of 130,000 square meters. The main building is 117 meters high and has 13 floors, including the Red Palace, the White House, the Buddhist temple, the sutra hall, the monk's house and the courtyard.

Wu Zhen

Walking into Wuzhen, there are white walls and tiles, small bridges and flowing water, and everything in front of us comes with a strong flavor of Chinese ink painting. The old houses with carved beams and painted buildings and stone alleys, which is known as the "land of fish and rice, the house of silk", have been the favorite of literati here since ancient times, and they are also the hometown of Mao Dun, a generation of literary giant. The ancient town has a rare water pavilion in the south of the Yangtze River, so it is called "the last pillow water family in China". "I've been here, but I never left", which is the feeling of Wuzhen in Rene Liu. Huang Lei also has this feeling. Because of his love, he and his little friends started the annual drama festival here, which also made Wuzhen a place where countless people dream about it. Some people say that Wuzhen, which has undergone unified transformation now, seems to be too delicate and has lost a historical vicissitudes. However, it can't be denied that the local cultures belonging to the ancient towns of wuyue have been completely preserved.
